SUBJECT: Quesiti Situazionali (RIPAM)

Focus:
- Workplace scenarios in public administration.
- Ethical behavior, responsibility, efficiency, collaboration.
- Decision-making under rules and procedures.

Structure:
- Present a realistic work scenario.
- Provide 3â€“4 possible actions.
- One action is clearly the MOST effective.
- One may be partially effective.
- One is ineffective or inappropriate.

Evaluation logic (implicit):
- Effectiveness
- Compliance with rules
- Responsibility and professionalism

Avoid:
- Extreme or unrealistic behaviors.
- Moral judgments or emotional language.
- Ambiguous scenarios without a clear best option.
